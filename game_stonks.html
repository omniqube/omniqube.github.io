STONKS is a simulation game in which the goal is to buy stocks and companies and gradually build your financial resources.

With the money you will be able to buy things and invest in real estate.

We plan to incorporate a story into the game, the events of which have a dynamic impact on actions. The player chooses dialogue options, and writes to different people, to move the story forward.
