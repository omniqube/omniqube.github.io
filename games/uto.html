Undertale: Orange is a fangame based on the 2015 hit "Undertale", the game where nobody has to die.

The Plot

18XX: You are Jeff, the soul of Bravery. Searching for a lost child, you find a cave in the Ebott Forest. Despite your hesitation, you are eager to start your adventure in the Underground. Don't forget: every decision, every attack and every move counts towards your ending. Good Luck.
